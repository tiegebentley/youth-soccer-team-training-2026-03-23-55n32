About Our Program

Youth Team Soccer Training was founded with a simple mission: to provide young athletes in the Cary, North Carolina area with access to world-class soccer coaching that develops not just their technical skills, but also their character and confidence. Our founder, Marcus, brought his passion for soccer and years of coaching experience to create a program that stands apart from traditional team sports. We believe that small group training environments allow coaches to provide personalized attention while maintaining the competitive energy that helps players thrive.

Our core values center on excellence, integrity, and player development. We're committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and positive environment where every young athlete can grow at their own pace. We emphasize not just winning, but the journey of improvement and the joy of playing the game we love. Through expert coaching, structured skill development, and a competitive yet supportive atmosphere, we help young soccer players build confidence, master technique, and develop the teamwork skills that will serve them throughout their athletic careers and beyond.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our training program

What age groups do you train?

We offer training programs for youth players ages 6 through 18, organized by age groups and skill levels. Our coaches tailor each session to be age-appropriate and developmentally suitable. Whether your child is just starting out or looking to improve their competitive level, we have a program that's right for them. We can discuss your child's current skill level and goals to recommend the best training group.

How often do training sessions occur?

We offer flexible training schedules to accommodate families' needs. Most players participate in 1-2 training sessions per week, with options for additional training during peak seasons. Each session is typically 60-90 minutes depending on age group and program level. We also offer special training camps and intensive programs during school breaks. You can choose the frequency that works best for your family's schedule and your player's development goals.

What if my child is a beginner with no prior experience?

Absolutely! We welcome beginners of all ages. Our beginner programs start with the fundamentals and build from there. Our coaches are experienced in working with players who have no prior soccer experience and create a welcoming, encouraging environment where they can learn at their own pace. We focus on building confidence alongside skill development, so new players feel comfortable and excited to learn. Many of our most dedicated players started as complete beginners.

What equipment do players need?

Players need soccer cleats or athletic shoes with good ankle support, shin guards, and a soccer ball. We provide cones, training equipment, and other facilities. For the first session, comfortable athletic clothing and any shoes you have are fine while you get the proper gear. We're happy to provide recommendations for affordable, quality equipment. Some of our training packages include equipment for new players to get started without a large upfront investment.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy! Simply reach out to us via email at marcus@smallgroupsoccer.com or visit our website to learn about current program offerings. We'll discuss your child's age, skill level, and goals to recommend the perfect training group. You can attend a trial session to see if it's a good fit before committing. We'll handle all the details and get your player on the field and improving their game in no time!
